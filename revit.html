LUKE SIMMONS RESUME WORK ABOUT ME

The Short Sands Beach House is designed to be a single family vacation home on the Oregon Coast. Based on a L shaped courtyard house, the layout is arranged and divided by functionality. The design features a unique slat wall system that encloses the living area to provide privacy, while still allowing light in and visibility out.
