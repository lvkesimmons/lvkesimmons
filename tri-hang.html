LUKE SIMMONS RESUME WORK

TRI-HANG

modular storage system

8 weeks

SKETCHING

The shape is designed to have a high production yield and tessellate easily.

SCALE MODELS

By thermoforming the foam I tested multiple types of pockets and connections.

FULL SCALE MODELS

On the full scale models an auxetic pattern is cut into the pocket to allow the foam to expand and create a greater volume.

PROCESS

The foam is laser cut and then the shape is thermoformed to create the pockets.

PROTOTYPE

Modules connect together with Fidlock SNAP to tessellate and create a structure
