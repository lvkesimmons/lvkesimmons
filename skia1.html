Skiing is a popular sport around the world, but it can also be very dangerous. If a skier goes down, it is often hard to pinpoint their exact location. It is even harder if there has been an avalanche. Experienced skiers often go down the hardest: trying more difficult runs and doing bigger tricks can result in worse injuries.

DIRECTION 1

Due to the sheer number of trees on the mountain, my first idea to place rings blocking where tree wells form was scrapped.

SKETCHING

Through ideation an aerodynamic camera and silicon case were designed.

MODELING

Iterating in 3D, both physically and digitally, helped establish the form of the camera.

CAMERA

By using the equipped gyroscope and accelerometer the camera tracks the users G force, watching for crashes or falls.

COLORS

The camera body comes in either white or black and the silicon case comes in three colors.

APP

The app is designed as a ski companion: it tracks motion data and establishes a social network on the mountain.

LAYOUT

Users are able to view the most important information with only one tap or swipe.
